Theophilus & Ana

I'm not leading from a stage. I'm leading from scars. I ignored every warning sign, made decisions from fear and confusion, and built a counterfeit version of what I thought marriage was supposed to be. God disciplined me. It was painful and necessary.

That's the story I put on the table — not to perform vulnerability, but because I've watched too many Christian men spend years trapped in the same confusion I was in, following advice that sounded spiritual but produced nothing.

What I've built since then — including the marriage you see between Ana and me — came from applying the whole counsel of God to dating. Not cherry-picked verses. Not church culture. Not red-pill cynicism. Scripture, taken seriously, applied with spine and joy.

I teach Kingdom dating: a clear, process-driven approach to formation, pursuit, discernment, and covenant — that actually works for men who take Jesus seriously.

What Kingdom Dating Means

  • Attraction is not sinful — a sexless, joyless marriage is not God's will
  • Faith without works is dead — including in dating and pursuit
  • "Waiting on God" is not a strategy. It's often fear wearing a spiritual mask.
  • Character formation comes before pursuit — in that order
  • Covenant is the goal, not dating for its own sake
  • The Bible has more to say about this than most churches teach
